# brand-balavu-products

**Brand:** Balavu  
**Domain:** https://balavu.com (apex) and https://www.balavu.com  
**Category:** Wing sail systems + sailing rig accessories for modular watercraft

## Purpose
This repository hosts the public-facing product definitions, documentation, and marketing-ready assets for **Balavu**.

Balavu focuses on **wing sails** and related rig components designed to integrate with modular boats and accessories across multiple sub-markets.

## Commerce & Memberships
Sales, memberships, and checkout will be handled by **WaterWolf** (private platform).  
This repo is intentionally **public** and contains only publishable product and documentation assets.

- `/catalog/` – product placeholders (to be populated as kits and accessories are finalized)
- `/LICENSE` and `/LICENSES/` – licensing scaffold (per your standard)
